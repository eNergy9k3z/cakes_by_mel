About Mel

Hi, I'm Mel! Baking has been my passion for over 15 years. What started as making birthday cakes for family has grown into a beloved local bakery.

Every cake, muffin, and cupcake is made from scratch using my grandmother's secret recipes, combined with modern techniques and premium ingredients.

I believe that every celebration deserves a cake that's as special as the moment itself. That's why I pour love into every single creation that leaves my kitchen.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order?

For custom cakes, we recommend ordering at least 2 weeks in advance. For our standard menu items, 3-5 days notice is perfect!

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We deliver throughout Aylesbury and surrounding areas. Delivery fees vary based on distance.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We can create gluten-free, dairy-free, and vegan options. Just let us know your requirements when ordering.
